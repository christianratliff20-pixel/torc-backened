HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. No configuration screens.

Detection starts the moment your video lands — there's nothing to set up first.

01

Upload or paste a link

Drag in a file, or paste a URL from YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, Instagram, X, or a podcast RSS feed. TORC auto-detects the source — no manual settings.

02

AI finds the highlights

The model scores every moment in the source video and ranks it by clip-worthiness, plotted directly on a visual timeline so you can see what it found.

03

Review, select, export

Pick the highlights you want from the queue and export — vertical-ready clips formatted for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.
